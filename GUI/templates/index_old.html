Welcome To Communique!


Communique is a technology which will allow you to selectively target specific species to express your gene of interest, while avoiding transfer of the genes to unwanted species within the microbiome.

In order to use this tool, you need to define your gene of interest and the bacterial species that should and shouldn't be able to express it. Then, you can define which expression parameters you would like to engineer.

For any additional information check out our user guide.
